About Invoxi

Fast, free invoice generator for freelancers, small teams, and side projects.

What is Invoxi?

Invoxi is a lightweight web app that helps you create professional invoices in minutes. No sign-up required, no heavy software. You fill your client details, items, taxes or discounts, and export a clean PDF that’s ready to send.

Who is it for?

  • Freelancers and consultants who need quick, polished PDFs.
  • Small businesses that don’t want complex accounting tools.
  • Side projects and startups that need a simple invoicing flow.

Key features

  • Logo, client & sender details, currency, due dates.
  • Tax, discount, delivery/shipping line, notes & signatures.
  • Import/export items as CSV; duplicate invoices.
  • Fast PDF export that prints beautifully.

Privacy & data

Invoxi runs in your browser. We don’t sell your data. Basic analytics may be used to improve the product (aggregate usage only). For full details, see our Privacy and Terms.

Roadmap

  • Saved invoice templates and quick client lists.
  • Multiple languages & RTL layout improvements.
  • Optional cloud backup (opt-in, encrypted).
