Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-07

Welcome to Invoxi. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Process

  • Usage data: basic analytics (pages visited, device, approximate location) to improve stability and UX.
  • Local-only data: invoice drafts, templates, and images (e.g., logo/signature) are stored in your browser’s localStorage. We do not receive this data unless you explicitly export and share it.

2. Cookies

We may use cookies to remember your preferences and improve performance. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

3. Advertising (Google AdSense)

We display ads via Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and/or other sites.

  • You can learn more about how Google uses data here: policies.google.com/technologies/ads.
  • Ad personalization controls: adssettings.google.com.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share aggregated, non-identifying analytics. Ad networks receive limited data necessary to show ads.

5. Data Retention

Local draft data remains on your device until you clear it. Server-side analytics, if any, are retained only as long as needed for security and performance.

6. Your Rights

You can clear local data from the app (e.g., “Clear profile”) or your browser storage. For other requests, contact us.

8. Changes
