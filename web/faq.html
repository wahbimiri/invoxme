Is Invoxi free?

Yes. You can create invoices and export printable PDFs for free.

Do you store my invoices on your servers?

No. Your drafts, templates, logo, and signature are saved in your browser’s localStorage. We don’t receive them unless you explicitly export and share.

Can I import/export CSV?

Yes. You can export items & totals to CSV, and import items from CSV using a simple column mapping (description, qty, price).

How are taxes, discounts, and delivery calculated?

subtotal = Σ(qty × price)
discountValue = subtotal × (discount%/100)
taxValue = (subtotal − discountValue) × (tax%/100)
total = subtotal − discountValue + taxValue + deliveryCharge

Can I add a signature and logo?

Yes. Upload your logo and an optional signature image. The signature block appears above the footer in the PDF.

Does it work offline?

The app runs inside your browser and saves drafts locally. You need Internet to load it the first time; PDF rendering is local.

Which payment statuses are supported?

Paid, Unpaid, Pay later, and Cash on delivery (COD). You can also type a custom status if needed.
