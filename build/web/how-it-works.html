Quick start

Open Invoxi

Go to invoxme.com and click Start New Invoice.

Add business & client info

Fill your company details (name, address, tax ID) and the client’s info. These fields appear at the top of the PDF.

Insert line items

Add products or services with quantity and price. You can reorder or remove items at any time.

Taxes, discount & delivery

Enable VAT or sales tax, add a discount (fixed or %), and delivery/shipping if needed. Totals update instantly.

Optional logo & signature

Upload your logo to brand the invoice and add a signature area to sign before sending.

Export the PDF

Click Export to generate a clean, printable PDF you can send to your client or archive for records.

Tips for accurate invoices

  • Use consistent invoice numbering (e.g., INV-2025-001).
  • Always include issue date, due date, and payment terms.
  • Add notes for bank transfer, reference, or PO number if required.
  • Keep client and tax IDs accurate to avoid delays.

FAQ

Is Invoxi free?

Yes. You can create and export invoices as PDF for free.

Can I add VAT or discounts?

Yes. You can enable VAT/sales tax, and add fixed or percentage discounts per invoice.

Where is my data stored?

We don’t permanently store your invoice data. Read more in our Privacy page.
