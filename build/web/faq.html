FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about Invoxi.

Does Invoxi store my invoice data?

No. Invoxi is privacy-friendly. Your data is generated in the browser and is not stored on our servers.

Can I export invoices as PDF?

Yes — a clean, print-ready PDF is generated with one click.

How do I add my logo?

Use the logo field in the editor to upload your image; it will appear in the PDF header.

Do you support taxes, discounts and delivery?

Yes. You can enable VAT/Tax, discount and delivery per invoice.

Which currencies and number formats are supported?

Type any currency code/symbol you prefer; formatting follows your inputs and locale.

Is right-to-left (Arabic) layout supported?

Yes. The UI supports RTL and Arabic content prints RTL in the PDF.

Can I import/export items via CSV?

Yes. Import items for quick invoice building and export them for reuse.

Does Invoxi support signatures?

Yes. Draw or upload a signature and it will be embedded in the PDF.

How can I save or share an invoice?

Download the PDF and share via email/apps. Keep your CSV to edit later.

Is Invoxi free?

Yes, it’s free to use.

How do I contact support?

Email hireready.store@proton.me or use the contact page.
